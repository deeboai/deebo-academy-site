## Tutor Notes
Keep the lesson active and oral. Students retain conjugation better when they repeatedly hear and use the form in complete sentences.

## In-Session Examples
Conjugate regular -er verbs, compare with a few irregular verbs, and build short context sentences with each form.

## Common Mistakes
Students often overgeneralize -er endings to irregular verbs and forget that pronunciation can hide written differences.

## Practice Problems
Conjugate eight verbs and translate five short present-tense sentences.

## Homework
Write a short paragraph about a daily routine using at least six present-tense verbs.

## Answer Key
The answer key should show correct endings, subject agreement, and one acceptable translation for each sentence.
