## Tutor Notes
Students often learn derivative rules procedurally before understanding what the number means. Slow down and keep interpreting the result in context.

## In-Session Examples
Estimate slopes from a graph, interpret derivative values in motion or growth contexts, and compare positive, negative, and zero derivative regions.

## Common Mistakes
Students mix up function values with derivative values and overuse the phrase increasing without connecting it to the sign of the derivative.

## Practice Problems
Interpret f'(2) for three contexts and explain what a derivative of zero means at a given point.

## Homework
Complete a worksheet that alternates between graphs, words, and symbolic derivative interpretation.

## Answer Key
Correct answers should state both the numeric slope meaning and the real-world interpretation when context is provided.
